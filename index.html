Growth in the music industry revolves around networking
It is how creators come across breakthrough opportunities, learn from other talented artists, and expand their creative outlook.
But small creators sparsely network
because they feel that it is tedious, disingenuous,”like a means to an end.” They sacrifice attending to this crucial component of their success.

We want to change that.

Networking can feel fun, organic and exciting.
Sprout is a space for creators to network through “Music Trains” by chaining short soundbites to each other’s work (inspired by the “Pass the Story” icebreaker from our pre-school memories).
